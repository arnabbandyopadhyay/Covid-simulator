Title: Covid simulator
Description: Covid simulator is an online tool designed for generating various possible pandemic scenarios. This code is build upon 'socialmixr' R package (https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/socialmixr/vignettes/socialmixr.html) and Rshiny App on social contact rates (SOCRATES, https://doi.org/10.1186/s13104-020-05136-9) which we integrated into our mathematical model to simulate pandemic scenarios.
